## Alchemy Alliance Affiliation

## 1. NON-DISCLOSURE
Affiliates shall not distribute materials created for the enrichment and benefit of other Alchemy Affiliates, including:
* Computer programs that compile code or instructions for generating in-game content, such as random map scripts.
* Computer programs that execute methods for content creation or organization (e.g., tools for map pooling or specification generation).
* Specifications in their native, alterable format (non-PDF files), except those intended as worksheets.

## 2. COMMUNICATION
Affiliates shall be accessible and responsive to each other:
* **Forum Membership:** Affiliates shall belong to Alchemy’s public forums at all times to remain contactable.
* **Responsiveness:** Affiliates should attempt to answer inquiries from other Affiliates within 48 hours, respecting that real-life obligations come first. Consistent failure to respond is considered a lack of professional respect.
* **Internal Solidarity:** Affiliates shall not publicly criticize other affiliates. Disputes must be handled internally.
* **Mutual Support:** Affiliates shall actively support one another (e.g., raiding streams featuring custom maps, promoting The Materials, participating in community events or discussions on map improvements).

## 3. PHILOSOPHY
Association with an Alchemy Affiliate should bring honor to both sides.

<ol type="A">
  <li><strong>BENEVOLENCE</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong> publicly insult or belittle other content creators, tournament hosts, players, or community members.
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Helpfulness:</strong> Try and help other affiliates where possible, such as sharing feedback on map designs or collaborating on improvements.</li>
      <li><strong>Prohibited Conduct:</strong> This includes derogatory comments on others' creations, use of profanity, name-calling, or dismissive taunts in discussions or streams.</li>
      <li><strong>Exceptions:</strong> Playful teasing is permitted if it is clearly in good spirit. Mitigating factors include:
        <ol type="a">
          <li>Frequent self-deprecation for humorous purposes.</li>
          <li>The recipient is a friendly acquaintance likely to share in the joke.</li>
          <li>The teasing is spontaneous in video content rather than directed via forum posts or chat.</li>
          <li>The feedback provides positive elements alongside the critique (e.g., "The map has great potential, but...").</li>
        </ol>
      </li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>HONESTY</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Plagiarize or copy others' custom maps, scripts, or specifications without proper credit and permission.</li>
      <li>Misrepresent their creations as original when derived from others' work.</li>
      <li>Distribute altered versions of official game content as their own.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>EQUALITY</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Invoke status, following, or expertise as a substitute for evidence-based reasoning in discussions.</li>
      <li>Advocate for preferential treatment or enforcement of guidelines based on popularity or contributions.</li>
      <li>Endorse content that excludes or is biased for or against any protected class of persons.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>PROFIT</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Sell or rent any materials created using Alchemy Alliance tools or methods (scripts, specifications, etc.).</li>
      <li>Publicly solicit gifts or broadcast personal financial goals related to AOE2 activities.</li>
      <li>Charge money for AOE2-related consultation or guidance.</li>
      <li>Participate in "Pay-to-Access" schemes or exchange content for status (e.g., subscriptions), excluding standard community donations for events.</li>
      <li>Sell promotional services or "shout-outs" for AOE2 content.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
</ol>
