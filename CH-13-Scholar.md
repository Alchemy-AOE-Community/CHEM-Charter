## Article

1. **Benefits**
    - Recognition for helping to develop more advanced tournament methods and quality of life improvements. Could include compensation models, tournament elo calculations, handicap assignments, etc.

2. **Requirements**
    - Be an Alchemy Affiliate per CH-06.
    - Have a free [GitHub Account](https://github.com).
    - Must understand any specification they are asked to review, or be willing to learn (particularly MTHD-XX, which tend to use advanced mathematics). Approves such specifications if they are technically correct or reasonable.

3. **Additional Notes**
    - This is a passive role. It is awarded so that anyone who is preparing intellectual property can know who to approach for "tough stuff" -- things that may be complicated, or require technical background to understand. No other responsibilities other than being a smart person who can ocassionally look over technical work in nerdy detail.
