## 1. OVERVIEW
This document outlines the leadership structure of the Alchemy AOE Alliance.

## 2. LEADERSHIP STRUCTURE
The key players in Alchemy leadership are:

<ol type="A">
  <li><strong>PRESIDENT</strong> – The leader of the Alchemy AOE Alliance:
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Responsibilities:</strong> The {President} acts autonomously to execute plans supporting the goals of the Alchemy AOE Alliance, and recruits the required team of Affiliates. He or she maintains ownership of:
        <ol type="a">
          <li><strong>The Alchemy AOE Server:</strong> The public forum where individuals of any character or affiliation may gather to participate with the Alchemy AOE Community.</li>
          <li><strong>The Alchemy AOE Github Repository:</strong> A location where intellectual property developed by Alchemy may be shared, both privately and publicly in furtherance of goals.</li>
          <li><strong>The Alchemy AOE GoogleDrive:</strong> A location where native specification files may be shared with limited visibility, for review/collaboration prior to publication.</li>
        </ol>
        The presidency of the Alchemy AOE Alliance aligns with ownership of all three above listed forums/repositories.
      </li>
      <li><strong>Activation/Deactivation:</strong> The {President} of the Alchemy AOE Alliance must be immune to external interests/influence, and the position is therefore appointed. If the current {President} no longer wishes to continue in the role, then he or she must notify the community no less than one month prior to resigning, and search for a replacement to appoint by transferring ownership of forums/repositories mentioned in the previous section. If a suitable replacement cannot be found before the resignation date then one month of notice shall be provided to all server members and contributors before all Alchemy AOE Alliance forums are deleted and the president may finally resign.</li>
      <li><strong>Impartiality:</strong> To prevent accusations of conflicting interests, the {President} cannot belong to any AOE2 clan or guild.</li>
      <li><strong>Lead-by-Example:</strong> The {President} must exemplify the general requirements for Alchemy Affiliation as defined in CH-009 and the Forum Code of Conduct defined in CH-005.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
</ol>
