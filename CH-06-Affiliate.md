## Article

1. **Selectivity** – Unlike general community membership, where anyone can be a member as long as they follow the rules, Alchemy Alliance Affiliation is awarded based on trust.

2. **Non-Disclosure** – {Affiliate}s shall not distribute materials created for the enrichment and benefit of other Alchemy Affiliates, including:
    * Computer programs that compile code or instructions for generating in-game content, such as random map scripts.
    * Computer programs that execute methods for competition preparation, such as tournament elo calculation or seeding.
    * Specifications in their native, alterable format (non-PDF files), except those intended as worksheets.

3. **Communication** – {Affiliate}s shall be accessible and responsive to each other:
    * **Forum Membership:** {Affiliate}s shall belong to Alchemy’s public forums at all times to remain contactable.
    * **Responsiveness:** {Affiliate}s should attempt to answer inquiries from other {Affiliate}s within 48 hours, respecting that real-life obligations come first. Consistent failure to respond is considered a lack of professional respect.
    * **Mutual Support:** {Affiliate}s shall actively support one another (e.g., raiding streams, promoting custom maps, participating in community events or discussions).

4. **Philosophy** – Association with an Alchemy {Affiliate} should bring honor to both sides.
    <ol type="A">
      <li><strong>Benevolence</strong> – {Affiliate}s shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
      <ol>
      <li>Publicly belittle or demean the character of others.</li>
      <li>Abandon professionalism through profanity, name-calling, or dismissive taunts.</li>
      <li>Tease without clear good spirit, self-deprecation, or positive feedback.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
      <li><strong>Honesty</strong> – {Affiliate}s shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
      <ol>
      <li>Plagiarize or copy others' content without proper credit and permission.</li>
      <li>Mislead seeding efforts of tournaments, to be matched against unworthy opponents.</li>
      <li>Make commitments within the scope of responsibilities that cannot be honored.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
      <li><strong>Equality</strong> – {Affiliate}s shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
      <ol>
      <li>Invoke status, following, or expertise as a substitute for evidence-based reasoning in discussions.</li>
      <li>Advocate for preferential treatment or enforcement of guidelines based on popularity or contributions.</li>
      <li>Create or endorse content that excludes or is biased for or against any protected class of persons.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
      <li><strong>Profit</strong> – {Affiliate}s shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
      <ol>
      <li>Publicly solicit gifts or broadcast personal financial goals related to AOE2 activities.</li>
      <li>Charge money for AOE2-related consultation or guidance.</li>
      <li>Sell promotional services or "shout-outs" for AOE2 content.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
    </ol>

5. **On/Off Boarding** –
    * **Process:** Any community member who wishes to become an {Affilate} need only message the {President}, who will respond within two weeks. Any {Affiliate} who wishes to leave may notify the {President}, who will adjust the proper roles within the same timeframe.
    * **Retain Secrets:**  Non-disclosure agreements apply to former {Affiliate}s.
