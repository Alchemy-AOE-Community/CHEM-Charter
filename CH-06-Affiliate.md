## Alchemy Alliance Affiliation

## 1. SELECTIVITY
Unlike general community membership, where anyone can be a member as long as they follow the rules, Alchemy Alliance Affiliation is awarded based on trust.

## 2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Affiliates shall not distribute materials created for the enrichment and benefit of other Alchemy Affiliates, including:
* Computer programs that compile code or instructions for generating in-game content, such as random map scripts.
* Computer programs that execute methods for competition preparation, such as tournament elo calculation or seeding.
* Specifications in their native, alterable format (non-PDF files), except those intended as worksheets.

## 3. COMMUNICATION
Affiliates shall be accessible and responsive to each other:
* **Forum Membership:** Affiliates shall belong to Alchemy’s public forums at all times to remain contactable.
* **Responsiveness:** Affiliates should attempt to answer inquiries from other Affiliates within 48 hours, respecting that real-life obligations come first. Consistent failure to respond is considered a lack of professional respect.
* **Internal Solidarity:** Affiliates shall not publicly criticize other affiliates. Disputes must be handled internally.
* **Mutual Support:** Affiliates shall actively support one another (e.g., raiding streams, promoting custom maps, participating in community events or discussions).

## 4. PHILOSOPHY
Association with an Alchemy Affiliate should bring honor to both sides.

<ol type="A">
  <li><strong>BENEVOLENCE</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Publicly belittle or demean the character of others.</li>
      <li>Abandon professionalism through profanity, name-calling, or dismissive taunts.</li>
      <li>Tease without clear good spirit, self-deprecation, or positive feedback.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>HONESTY</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Plagiarize or copy others' content without proper credit and permission.</li>
      <li>Misrepresent their creations or content as original when derived from others' work.</li>
      <li>Distribute altered versions of official game content as their own.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>EQUALITY</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Invoke status, following, or expertise as a substitute for evidence-based reasoning in discussions.</li>
      <li>Advocate for preferential treatment or enforcement of guidelines based on popularity or contributions.</li>
      <li>Create or endorse content that excludes or is biased for or against any protected class of persons.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <br>
  <li><strong>PROFIT</strong> – Affiliates shall <strong>NOT</strong>:
    <ol>
      <li>Publicly solicit gifts or broadcast personal financial goals related to AOE2 activities.</li>
      <li>Charge money for AOE2-related consultation or guidance.</li>
      <li>Sell promotional services or "shout-outs" for AOE2 content.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
</ol>

## 5. ON/OFF BOARDING

* **Process:** Any community member who wishes to become an Affilate need only message the {President}, who will respond within two weeks. Any Affiliate who wishes to leave may notify the {President}, who will adjust the proper roles within the same timeframe.
* **Retain Secrets:**  Non-disclosure agreements apply to former Affiliates.
