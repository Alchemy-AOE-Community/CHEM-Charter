## Article
  
1. **Overview** – This document outlines the Code of Conduct for participation in Alchemy AOE Alliance Forums, including servers and online repositories owned by Alchemy. 
    
2. **Philosophy** – The Alchemy AOE Community operates on the premise that a philosophically diverse group of people can unite over a common interest. No moral judgment is made for or against the character of anyone who participates in Alchemy forums, but a Code of Conduct ensures that everyone can feel welcome.
        
3. **Code of Conduct** – The following terms apply:
    <ol type="A">
      <li><strong>Just Be Nice:</strong> Bullying, harassment, and toxicity will not be tolerated. Follow the golden rule: "Do to others only what you would have them do to you." There's something of value in everyone, and we want them to offer it as an attraction to our server. All are welcome in our public spaces, as long as they follow the rules.</li>
      <li><strong>Forbidden Topics:</strong> Written discussion of politics or religion is not allowed. Alchemy forums were created to fulfill the objectives of the community. They are not platforms for attempting to persuade others of political/religious opinions.</li>
      <li><strong>No Vulgarity:</strong> Content that is considered "Not Safe for Work (NSFW)" is forbidden. This includes the imagery of a user's discord profile avatar, as well as their name. Profanity/crudity are also prohibited.</li>
      <li><strong>Excessive Pinging:</strong> Individuals should be mindful of the number of notifications they send to other server users. Pings like @everyone or @here are reserved for important announcements only.</li>
      <li><strong>Relevance:</strong> Respect the time of other server users by writing in the channel that best describes what you have to say.</li>
      <li><strong>Lawkeeping:</strong> Do not encourage others to break the law. Respect the decisions of admins/moderators. Moderation appeals should be sent privately through direct message.</li>
      <li><strong>English Only:</strong> Alchemy forums are English-speaking. When everyone speaks English we can enforce the rules.</li>
    </ol>
    
4. **Moderation** – No targeted action may be taken against any member of the Alchemy AOE Community without proof that the Code of Conduct was violated. 
    
    <ol type="A">
      <li><strong>Tiers of Intervention</strong> – The following types of action could be taken:
      <ol type="1">
      <li><strong>Message Cleanup:</strong> The {Moderator} performs basic server cleanup. Examples could include:
      <ol type="a">
      <li>Duplicate messages sent in tandem.</li>
      <li>Indiscernible messages without context (E. G. “covfefe”).</li>
      <li>Flagging Messages sent for the sole purpose of alerting a {Moderator} to a potential rule violation.</li>
      <li>Messages which are left over after a public discussion on moderation.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
      <li><strong>Message Deletion:</strong> Violation of the code of conduct results in deletion and private notification.</li>
      <li><strong>User Time-Out:</strong> Temporary removal of posting abilities if someone is in the “heat of the moment.”</li>
      <li><strong>User Ban:</strong> Removal from the server for extreme or repeated violations.</li>
      </ol>
      </li>
      <li><strong>INDEPENDENT EVALUATION</strong> – Except in cases of NSFW, {Moderator}s consider only the history a user has with the Alchemy AOE Community.</li>
      <li><strong>APPEALS</strong> – Moderation decisions may only be appealed through the process of CH-05.</li>
    </ol>
